META PIRATES NFT COLLECTION

Meta Pirates is a private collection of 8,888 NFTs of exclusive pirates living in the Ethereum blockchain.

Each piece is a unique piece of 3D art with a collection of over 150 features. Our goal is to create a strong, ambitious community that - like the pirates - is ready to conquer the lands and seas of the metaverse with us.

Meta Pirates aspires to connect with a philosophy of conquerors. You will be connected with every member of this crew, each having the same vision as you: to seize the riches of web 3.0.

EVERYTHING STARTS HERE...

One day, a pirate who had left to discover new lands, comes back claiming to have found a dimensional portal, which could bring the crew to new lands & seas: the metaverse.

After long investigations and research, the crew learns of untold treasures to be found in this new world. However, enemies had already managed to gain access to the gates leading to this piece of paradise.

Excited and bored with the terrestrial continents, the 8,888 pirates decide to lift the ink and go in search of the portals that can teleport them into the metaverse.

The RoadMap

1

Phase 1: Welcome aboard!

Our priority is to develop an active and cohesive crew to ensure the success of our collection.

We want our crew to be made up of pirates who share our values and uphold the pirate code. The quality of our designs is also an essential part of our collection, so we have chosen elite avatars to best represent our pirates.

We will announce our release date as soon as we are satisfied with the quality and the technical aspects of the project.

2

Phase 2: Crew privileges and Merchandising

Our NFT holders will be invited to exclusive events in the world of cinema, art, watches, entrepreneurship, fashion and parties.

Meta-pirate crew members will have access to exclusive premium merchandise. This will not be limited to simple items: each member will have an original piece customized with their NFT number.

3

Phase 3: HOLD TO EARN

After the reveal, your pirate goes to conquer the blockchain. He will scour the treasures of the blockchain to bring you between 1 and 3 treasure chests per month.

In these chests are big rewards that depend on the rarity of your Meta Pirates. Among the rewards, you can find etherums, exclusive meta-pirates merch and who knows, maybe even elixirs that can increase the value of your NFT...

There are 3 types of treasure: legendary, epic and rare.

4

Phase 4: Heart of pirates: the soul of the Ocean

Pirates live on the ocean: it allows us to sail, to feed, and to conquer, so we must preserve it.

The ocean offers unforgettable moments to all pirates worthy of the name. That is why we want to take care of it. For this, 70,000 dollars will be donated to an organization that fights pollution of the seas and oceans.

The association will be chosen by the DAO.

5

Phase 5: Play To Earn

Experience the battlefields through your NFT!

Welcome to the game "Meta Pirate P2E" where you have the possibility to build your crew and conquer your enemies' lands.

To balance the game economy, PvE and PvP modes will be available, as well as a prop market. Players can monetize their gaming experiences by catching their enemies' tokens and trading their MPR tokens for Dollars.

6

Phase 6: Conquering the Oceans of the Metaverse.

We've assembled a crew of determined pirates with a thirst for conquest. Now it's time to establish our influence through the oceans of the metaverse!

To do this, we need to gather the Meta Pirate members. For this purpose, we will make the "Black Chain" available to our crew.

Our galleon will be available in the metaverse and singular to our crew members. On this galleon, our crew members will have access to exclusive activities (casino, games, etc.) and art galleries for our legendary NFT's.

It will allow masterminds on the WeB 3.0 to exchange their latest business opportunities and ideas, have a good time and maintain a tight community.

METAPIRATES LOOT

Your pirates have a real purpose! METAPIRATES will plunder the treasures of the blockchain and bring them back to their owners!

There will be 3 different rTarities for chests: rare, epic and legendary. The rarer your Meta Pirates are, the more likely they are to bring you a legendary chest.

REWARDS

The chests are full of incredible rewards! Among them, you can find boosts, etherum, xp, merchandizing, cruise tickets... and who knows, if you are lucky maybe even an elexir allowing your METAPIRATES to mutate and increase its value, its in-game abilities, its stacking power...

DISTRIBUTION

Pirates will bring their holder 2 treasure chests per month in the first season.

If your pirate was obtained via White List, that's up to 4 chests per month!

FAQ

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for "Non-Fungible Token". It's a specific, unique, digital item with blockchain-managed ownership that users can buy, own and trade.

What is Meta Pirates?

Meta Pirates is an NFT collection consisting of 8,888 unique 3D generative art. Owning a Meta Pirates NFT allows you to benefit from opportunities related to the crypto and business world.

When will the official Meta Pirate launch take place?

he official launch date is to exclusively be announced on Discord and our social media. Our plan is to take over the Metaverse by the end of January 2022.
